General Information

  • Origin: Siberia, Russia
  • Size: Medium (20-24 inches tall, 35-60 pounds)
  • Coat: Thick double coat, often in black and white or gray and white
  • Lifespan: 12-15 years

Breed Characteristics

Siberian Huskies are known for wolf-like looks and piercing blue or multicolored eyes.

They are highly energetic and were originally bred by the Chukchi people of Siberia as sled dogs.

Size

  • Adult Males: 20-27 kg, 53-60 cm tall
  • Adult Females: 16-23 kg, 50-56 cm tall

Temperament

  • Friendly, alert and energetic
  • Intelligent but independent and stubborn

They are known for being good with children and other dogs.

Care Requirements

  • Exercise: High energy levels require daily exercise
  • Grooming: Regular brushing to manage shedding
  • Training: Consistent and firm training is necessary

Fun Facts

  • Siberian Huskies can run up to 28 miles per hour.
  • They have a unique vocalization that is a mix between a howl and a yodel.
  • Huskies are known for their ability to escape, so secure fencing is essential.

Quick Facts
